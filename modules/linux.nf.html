Module linux.nf

Linux kernel constants exposed under linux.nf.

Values are populated at build time from the kernel headers.

Tables

action Netfilter hook verdicts.
arp Netfilter hook positions in the ARP family.
br Netfilter hook positions in the BRIDGE family.
br.pri Netfilter hook priorities in the BRIDGE family.
inet Netfilter hook positions in the INET family.
ip.pri Netfilter hook priorities in the IP family.
netdev Netfilter hook positions in the NETDEV family.
proto Netfilter protocol families.
